Privacy

Privacy Notice

How information from this site is handled.

This site collects only the information you choose to share through the contact form on the homepage: your name (optional), your email address, and your message.

What I do with it

Your message is sent directly to my personal email inbox. I read every message and reply when I can. Your email address is only used to respond to you. I will not add you to any mailing list, share your information with third parties, or use it for marketing.

Who else sees it

  • Resend — the email service that delivers your message from this site to my inbox.
  • Fly.io — the host this site runs on, which sees your IP address as part of normal web traffic.
  • No one else.

Cookies and tracking

This site uses no analytics, no cookies, and no tracking pixels. Your visit is not recorded.

Removing your information

If you'd like me to delete a message you've sent, just reply to my response with the request, or send a new message through the contact form on the homepage.
